Takeaways

  • Counting multisets (discussed in the preceding chapter) counts the number of ways to distribute rrr objects into nnn types. Once you have chosen a multiset, you now must choose which objects will be which type according to the distribution. Multinomial coefficients count the number of ways to do so.
  • Multinomial coefficients are generalizations of binomial coefficients for k>2k>2k>2, so many theorems “come up,” including the recurrence relation and the multinomial theorem.
