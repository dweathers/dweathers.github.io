The relation is reflexive because a∨a=aa \vee a = aa∨a=a for all elements aaa of a Boolean algebra. So, a≤aa \le aa≤a.*

Finally, the relation is transitive. Suppose a≤ba \le ba≤b and b≤cb \le cb≤c. Then, a∨b=ba \vee b = ba∨b=b and b∨c=cb \vee c = cb∨c=c. Then,
